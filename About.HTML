About Lavatory War Roleplay (LWRP)

Our Story

The LWRP server immerses players in the fictional world of Lavatory War, where alliances are forged, battles are fought, and narratives unfold. It is a roleplay space for creativity, conflict, and camaraderie.

Our Community

We’re a community of roleplayers who respect each other’s boundaries and work together to create exciting, evolving storylines. Whether you’re a veteran roleplayer or a newcomer, you’ll find a place here.
